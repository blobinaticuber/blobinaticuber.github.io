How I got started

Some time in March 2016 I saw my mom playing with a Rubik's Cube, and we decided to race to see who could build a side of a single colour the fastest. She always won, so one day I looked up a full tutorial on how to solve the cube, and pretended like I had figured it out myself to show off to my mom. My first ever timed solve was about 10 minutes.

Hypercubing

In 2021, I learned of the 4-Dimensional Rubik's Cube. It took me a week to solve it by following a written guide online. Since then I have (briefly) held the 3x3x3x3 speedsolving world record, helped create the hypercubing.xyz wiki, solved the 5D and 6D cubes, and helped foster the community on the Discord server.

My most viewed video (The Quest to Build a 4D Rubik's Cube) sitting at 800,000+ views.
