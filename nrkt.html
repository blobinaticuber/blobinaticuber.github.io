nRKT tool

This tool finds {1-4}RKT cancels for 2-gen Rubik's Cube algorithms. 4flip means to do the rotation that looks like it swaps the R and U layers of all layers along the 4th axis besides the one that you're doing RKT on. On 3^4, this can be notated as {1..2}OUR.
