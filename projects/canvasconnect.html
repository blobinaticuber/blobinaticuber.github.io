Repository

Features

  • School-wide announcements and events
  • Discover clubs and make new friends
  • Talk to peers in your classes and form study groups
  • Fully functioning messaging platform so you can talk to members for group projects

About

Many students experience isolation, and lack resources to connect on campus. We want to transform Canvas from a solitary academic tool into a vibrant community hub for social good. Canvas Connect is a fully functioning browser extension adding on to the Canvas platform where you can learn about all the things happening on campus.
