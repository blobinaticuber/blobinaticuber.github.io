Funny Clocks

Funny Clocks is a collection of alternative methods of timekeeping. Hover over each one for more information.

24 Hour Time

::

The day is divided into 24 hours, which have 60 minutes, which have 60 seconds.

12 Hour Time

::

Take 24 hour time, and divide it into two 12-hour long segments. One is denoted by AM and the other PM.

Seconds since midnight

The total number of seconds that have elapsed since last midnight. It counts from 0 to 86400.

Metric Time

::

The day is divided into 10 hours, which have 100 minutes, which have 100 seconds.

Binary Time

The day is divided into 216 parts, and counted up using the binary number system.

Hexadecimal Time

The day is divided into 216 parts, and counted up using the hexadecimal number system.
